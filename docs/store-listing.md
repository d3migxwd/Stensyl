## Chrome Web Store

### Short description (max 132 characters)

Overlay design screenshots on live pages to compare your UI to the design—like a stencil—without juggling Figma and the browser.

*(128 characters)*

### Detailed description

Stensyl overlays a design screenshot on top of the page you're building so you can compare implementation to design—like a stencil—without switching between Figma (or another design tool) and your HTML/CSS in separate tabs.

**Why it helps**

As a frontend developer, it's easy to lose fidelity on padding, margins, spacing, and overall layout. Stensyl helps you see what matches, what's off, and where the UI is drifting from the design. Upload a screenshot, align it on the page, and adjust opacity to spot differences.

**How it works**

1. Click the Stensyl icon and choose Start.
2. Upload a PNG or JPEG of your design (from Figma, Sketch, or any tool).
3. The overlay appears on the current page. Drag to position it, use the controls to change opacity and scale.
4. Compare your live implementation to the design side by side.
5. Stop when you're done.

All data stays local. No accounts, no telemetry.
