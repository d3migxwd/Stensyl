# Privacy Policy — Stensyl

**Last updated:** March 2025

Stensyl is a browser extension that overlays design screenshots on web pages so you can compare your implementation to the design. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data We Collect

**We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on our servers.** Stensyl does not use accounts, analytics, or telemetry.

## Data Stored Locally

Stensyl stores one item on your device using the browser’s local storage (`chrome.storage.local`):

- **Last uploaded screenshot** — The image you upload (PNG or JPEG) is stored locally as a base64 string so it can be shown as an overlay. This data never leaves your device.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. Stensyl does not send information to any external servers.

## Data Retention

The screenshot is stored until you:

- Click **Stop Stensyl** (which clears it), or  
- Uninstall the extension, or  
- Clear the extension’s storage in your browser settings.

## Permissions

Stensyl requests:

- **Storage** — To save the screenshot locally on your device.
- **Active tab** — To show the overlay on the page you’re viewing when you use the extension.
- **Access to http/https pages** — So the overlay can run on normal web pages (including localhost) where you’re building UI.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, open an issue in the [Stensyl repository](https://github.com/d3migxwd/Stensyl) or contact the maintainer.
